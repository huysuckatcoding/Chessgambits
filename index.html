WHAT IS GAMBIT?

A gambit (from ancient Italian gambetto, the act of tipping someone with the leg to make him fall) is a chess opening in which a player sacrifices material with the aim of achieving a subsequent positional advantage. The word "gambit" is also sometimes used to describe similar tactics used by politicians or business people in a struggle with rivals in their respective fields, for example "The early election was a risky gambit by Theresa May".
